# Agentic — Design Agency Template

A free, single-page **Framer** template for creative studios and design agencies. Bold editorial layout with sections for case-study work, team, awards, and services.

## Overview

Agentic is a polished, content-ready agency site you can remix and rebrand. It ships as a single scrolling page with anchored navigation plus a few standalone routes (work detail pages and legal pages). The aesthetic is editorial and high-contrast, aimed at studios that want their work to lead.

## Sections

| Section | Anchor | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Hero | `#hero-section` | Headline statement + intro copy |
| Work | `#work-section` | Featured case studies (Artikle, Flowpath, Kudos) |
| About | `#about-section` | Studio story + team carousel |
| Services | `#services` | Six service offerings |
| Awards | — | Chronological awards list |
| Contact | `#footer-background` | Email, booking link, social, legal |

## Routes

- `/` — main single-page site
- `/works` — full project index
- `/works/artikle`, `/works/flowpath`, `/works/kudos` — case-study detail pages
- `/legal/privacy-policy`, `/legal/cookie-policy`, `/licensing` — legal pages

## Getting started

1. Open the [remix link](https://framer.link/zbgYFJM) to copy the template into your own Framer account.
2. Rebrand: swap the `AGENTIC®` wordmark, colors, and typography in the Framer canvas.
3. Replace placeholder content — team members, work items, awards, and service copy.
4. Update contact details (the template uses `hello@agentic.com`, a Calendly booking link, and social links).
5. Publish from Framer to your own domain.
